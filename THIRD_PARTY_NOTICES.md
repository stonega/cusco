## pi-computer-use

Cusco's computer-use protocol and state-scoped observe/act workflow were
adapted from [iamcheyan/pi-computer-use](https://github.com/iamcheyan/pi-computer-use),
revision `0b3bff3b42e17b662cc505a8c11c3d064d907b62` (2026-07-13).

Cusco does not vendor the upstream macOS, Windows, Node.js, Pi extension, or
cross-platform backend-selection layers. The port in `src/computerUse/` and
`data/gnome-shell/extensions/` is Linux-only and uses GNOME Shell/Mutter APIs
directly for Wayland window discovery, capture, input, workspace switching,
and the emergency stop indicator.
